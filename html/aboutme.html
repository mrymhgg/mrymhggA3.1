A Background About Me

Hey, I'm Maryam Haggag, 19 years young, and a Virgo, born on September 20, 2004. I’m Palestinian, but I grew up in Abu Dhabi, the UAE's capital, which was pretty cool. I'm the youngest in the family, with three older siblings who’ve all gone through Carleton University. Guess what? I’m walking the same path, aiming to snag my Bachelor's in Arts, focusing on Interactive Multimedia and Design. Right now, I'm in my first year, and honestly, it's been a mix of cool and challenging, especially with the tough coding and math parts. My big goal? To get awesome grades and make my family super proud—parents, grandparents, you name it. But, being real, it’s not always easy. Ottawa can get super boring and lonely compared to the lively vibes back in the UAE. Missing my family, the culture, and my tight-knit circle of friends (and my cat!) can get pretty tough. But, I’m here following my dad and siblings' footsteps, determined to make the best of it. Oh, and a little side note: web development is turning out to be my favorite class. It kinda makes me believe in myself more, knowing that I can tackle this challenge head-on.
